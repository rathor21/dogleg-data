About

Golf's conventional wisdom, run through real data, at every handicap.

Tour has ShotLink and Data Golf. The 8-to-30 handicap playing a $40 muni gets hand-me-down advice built from tour numbers. Dogleg Data serves that golfer.

The verification standard

Every claim on this site names its source. Every modeled number gets labeled modeled, on every chart, every time, no exceptions. Analyses go through peer review before publishing, and the findings get published too, including what broke and how it got fixed.
